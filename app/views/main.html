Billomat Invoice Downloader


A simple utility to download invoices from Billomat.


This utility helps you to download Billomat invoices easy and fast. Currently this is a very slow process when using Billomat. It is free of charge and your data (API key & Billomat ID) is only stored on your client and can be deleted.



Where do I find my API key?
  1. Log into your Billomat account
  2. click on your name (top right)
  3. check API access
  4. copy and paste the key

Where do I find my Billomat ID?
When you are logged in to your Billomat account you will see the ID as part of the URL e.g. "superdupercompany.billomat.net". In this example your ID would be "supderdupercompany".
